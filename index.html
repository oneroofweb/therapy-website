Procedure for Relaxing Body Massage

A body massage at home is one of the best ways to relax your body, remove stress, and calm your mind without going to the spa. Here is our professional procedure.

1

Prepare a Relaxing Environment

Prior to beginning, we create a peaceful environment. We ensure the room is clean, noise-free, and has good airflow. Lighting is dimmed or softened with lamps, and relaxing music is played. The temperature is adjusted to be slightly warm to help the body relax completely.

2

Pick the Proper Massage Oil

We select oils like coconut, almond, or olive oil to ensure smooth movements and skin nourishment. The oil is warmed slightly before application to improve blood circulation, deeply relax muscles, and soothe the skin. We always perform a patch test for sensitive skin.

3

Head and Neck Massage

We gently begin with the head and neck. Small circular movements on the scalp, temples, and forehead help relieve mental stress. We then move to the neck and shoulders, applying gentle pressure with thumbs and palms to reduce headaches caused by long working hours.

4

Shoulder, Arms & Hand Massage

Using long strokes from shoulders to arms, we gently knead muscles to release tightness. We carefully massage the palms and fingers to target specific pressure points, promoting comfort and greatly improving blood circulation.

5

Back Massage for Deep Relaxation

The back is integral to a full body massage. We use longitudinal strokes from the lower to upper back and rub around the spine and scapula in a clockwise fashion. This reduces back discomfort, muscle stiffness, and stress while improving posture.

6

Leg and Foot Massage

We treat the legs with significant pressure and upward strokes on thighs and calves. A foot massage follows to relax muscles, improve sleep quality, and activate pressure points related to internal organs, decreasing overall fatigue.

7

Final Touch & Relaxation

Light strokes are passed over the entire body to aid oil absorption and create a feeling of completeness. We wrap you in a warm towel and allow you to rest for a few minutes to fully absorb the benefits of the therapy.

After Care

We recommend a shower to wash away excess oil. Drinking warm water or herbal tea helps eliminate released toxins. It is best to avoid a heavy meal immediately after and continue resting to let the massage work effectively.
